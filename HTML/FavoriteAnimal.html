Giant Panda

Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca

What are they?

Panda bears are black and white in color. They are usually found in bamboo forests in the mountains of central China.

Diet

Panda Bears sometimes eat small animals and fish, but 99% of their diet mostly comes from eating bamboo.

Lifestyle

Panda bears do not hibernate. When winter comes, they just find a warmer place to stay and eat bamboo.
