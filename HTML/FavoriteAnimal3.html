Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca

What are they?

Panda bears are black and white in color. They are usually found in bamboo forests in the mountains of central China.

Diet

Panda Bears sometimes eat small animals and fish, but 99% of their diet mostly comes from eating bamboo.

Lifestyle

Panda bears live mostly on their own. They also spend most of their days resting and eating.
Panda bears sleep in many positions. They like lying on their back, their sides, and their stomach. When they sleep in trees, they put their hind paws on the tree and use their front paws to shade their eyes.
