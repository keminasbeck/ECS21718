Rosa Parks

Mother of the Civil Rights Movement


Rosa Parks was a Civil rights activist.

When she was on a bus, she refused to give up her seat for a white person.
Her action helped influence the nation to end segregation laws.

Achievements
  1. Spingarn Medal in 1979
  2. Golden Plate Awards in 1995
  3. Presidential Medal of Freedom in 1996
  4. Congressional Gold Medal in 1999
  5. NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series in 2000
